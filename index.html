SDSLabs Recruitments: Now open for first year.
SDSLabs is a tech student body organization that does cool stuff at IIT-Roorkee. We're innovating in lots of technical areas and aim to make lives easier for everyone. Help us promote innovation.
WE ARE LOOKING FOR AWESOME PROGRAMMERS & DESIGNERS!

Programmers

At SDSLabs, we are continously working on many applications and ideas. We want people with a passion to code, with strong skills in any programming language. You should have a passion to learning, and be a fast-learner. We will be holding a written test in early january for recruiting programmers. The exam will consist of 4 sections - Logic, Programming, HTML/CSS, and Creative.

Designers

We are looking for designers with a passion to design products. This involves great Photoshop/CorelDraw skills, and good communication skills. If you feel like you belong here, send us a mail at contact@sdslabs.co.in with your previous works attached. Keep the subject as SDSLabs Recruitments and don't forget to mention your name and branch.
