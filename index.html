SDSLabs' Recruitment for first year: Details to be announced soon
SDSLabs is a student organization that makes cool stuff at IIT Roorkee. We are innovating in a lot of technical areas and aim to make lives easier for everyone. Help us promote innovation.
WE ARE LOOKING FOR AWESOME PROGRAMMERS & DESIGNERS!

Programmers

At SDSLabs, we are continously working on interesting applications and ideas. We want people with a passion for code and strong skills in programming. You should have a willingness to learn and be a fast-learner. We will be conducting a written test in January for recruiting programmers. The exam will consist of 4 sections - Logic, Programming, HTML/CSS, and Creative.

Designers

We are looking for designers with a passion to design products. This involves great Photoshop/CorelDraw skills, and good communication skills. The problem statement and instructions for designers will be available in January.
