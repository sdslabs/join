Why SDSLabs?

Last year saw 12 selections from SDSLabs in Google Summer of Code. We were the Global Winners of CSAW ESC this year. and also stood 2nd in India and 6th among the World UG in CSAW CTF 2021. Our members, with their remarkable skills in various fields like web development, information security, machine learning and game development, landed jobs in companies like Google, Microsoft, Adobe, Goldman Sachs & Facebook. Our alumni base ranges from the co-founders of Razorpay to the researchers in universities like Georgia Tech, NYU & MIT.
