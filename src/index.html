Why SDS?

Last year saw 8 selections from SDSLabs in the Google Summer of Code. With clean sweeps in Microsoft Build The Shield (National ranks 1 and 2) and Code.fun.do (3 out of top 4 teams), we also won the NYU CSAW CTF. Our members, with their remarkable skills in various fields like web development, information security, machine learning, game development, landed jobs in companies like Google, Microsoft, Adobe, Goldman Sachs & Facebook. Our alumni base ranges from the co-founders of Razorpay to the researchers in universities like Georgia Tech, NYU & MIT.
